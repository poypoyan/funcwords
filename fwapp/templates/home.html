Welcome!


This is an expanding compilation of Function Words in Philippine Languages (FWPHL). Function words are words that provide grammatical structure to a sentence, rather than being representations of objects or phenomena. These are pronouns, case markers, affixes, conjunctions, particles, etc. This project aims to collect these words from over a hundred languages in the Philippines, to better see the similarities and differences among those.
